Criteria Details
Shape Rounded/pyramid-like top, cup-like flowers
Color Very dark purple leaves and branches, pink flowers
Weaknesses many, including caterpillars, silver leaf, and aphids
Maintenance high maintenance levels required, including pruning after the flowers emerge
Fruit Fruit (plums) grow from the flowers, which can attract birds and other animals

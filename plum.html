Fact Sheet


Criteria Details
Shape large flower, long “trumpet” shape in center
Color commonly white and yellow
Blooming Season early to mid spring
Growth Conditions various soil moisture conditions, full or partial sunshine
Growth Environment many possible environments
Resistance resistant against drought, deer, rabbit
Maintenance low maintenance levels required
Side Effects causes discomfort when eaten, irritates the skin
